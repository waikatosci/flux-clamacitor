Model Scope
This GUI implements the four-flux mass balance framework (Eqs. 1–7) from Section 2.4 of the companion paper. It is intended for scenario exploration and parameter sensitivity analysis. Positive fluxes only are used in population scaling; dissolution events and negative fluxes are excluded consistent with the published methodology. The Waikato River system is systematically undersaturated with respect to calcite (SIcc < 0), ruling out abiotic Ca removal.
